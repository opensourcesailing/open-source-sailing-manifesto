# ⚓ Open Source Sailing Principles

## The values that guide our projects and community

Open Source Sailing is built on a foundation of openness, collaboration and respect for the sailing community.

These principles guide every project, contribution and decision.

---

# 1. Open by Design ⚓

We believe technology should be transparent and understandable.

Whenever possible, our projects should include:

- Open-source code
- Open documentation
- Open communication protocols
- Accessible design information

Users should know how their systems work.

---

# 2. Repair Instead of Replace 🔧

Marine equipment should be built to last.

We support:

- Repairability
- Reuse of existing equipment
- Long-term maintenance
- Hardware upgrades instead of unnecessary replacement

A 30-year-old instrument should have a chance to continue sailing.

---

# 3. Standards Over Lock-In 🌍

We support open standards and interoperability.

Examples:

- NMEA0183
- NMEA2000
- Signal K
- MQTT
- Open data formats

Technology should communicate freely.

---

# 4. Share Knowledge 🤝

Knowledge becomes stronger when shared.

We encourage:

- Clear documentation
- Tutorials
- Educational content
- Community contributions
- Sharing solutions and lessons learned

Failures are also valuable knowledge.

---

# 5. Sailor First 🚢

Technology exists to support sailors.

Projects should prioritize:

- Reliability
- Simplicity
- Safety
- Practical use onboard

Technology should serve the sailor, not the other way around.

---

# 6. Sustainable Innovation 🌱

Innovation does not always mean replacing old systems.

We focus on:

- Extending equipment lifetime
- Reducing electronic waste
- Creating efficient solutions
- Making technology accessible

---

# 7. Community Driven 🌊

Open Source Sailing belongs to everyone who contributes.

We welcome:

- Different experience levels
- Different sailing backgrounds
- Different technical skills

Everyone can help improve the ecosystem.

---

# 8. Build in Public 📡

We document the journey.

This includes:

- Design decisions
- Experiments
- Prototypes
- Testing
- Successes
- Failures

The process is part of the project.

---

# Our Promise

Every Open Source Sailing project should aim to be:

⚓ Open  
🔧 Repairable  
🌍 Sustainable  
🤝 Collaborative  
🚢 Useful

---

**Code. Sail. Share. Connect.**
