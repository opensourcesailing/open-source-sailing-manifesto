# ⚓ Open Source Sailing Manifesto

## Code. Sail. Share. Connect.

Open Source Sailing is an open-source initiative dedicated to making marine technology open, repairable and accessible.

We believe that sailing technology should not be limited by closed ecosystems, expensive upgrades or proprietary systems.

Through open hardware, open software and community collaboration, we aim to connect sailors, makers, developers and engineers around the world.

---

# Our Vision

A future where every sailor can understand, repair, modify and improve the technology onboard their vessel.

We envision a world where marine electronics are:

- Open
- Interoperable
- Affordable
- Repairable
- Community-driven

---

# Our Mission

Open Source Sailing develops and promotes open solutions for marine technology.

We focus on:

- Open marine hardware
- Open-source navigation solutions
- Linux-based onboard systems
- Open marine data
- ESP32 and embedded electronics
- SDR communication technologies
- Boat automation
- Educational documentation

---

# Our Principles

## ⚓ Open

Technology should be transparent and accessible. Users should understand how their systems work.

## 🔧 Repairable

Marine equipment should be designed to last, be maintained and be repaired instead of replaced unnecessarily.

## 🌍 Sustainable

Extending the lifetime of existing boats and equipment reduces waste and encourages responsible innovation.

## 🤝 Collaborative

Knowledge grows when people share ideas, improvements and solutions.

## 🚢 Sailor-focused

Technology should empower sailors instead of locking them into closed ecosystems.
