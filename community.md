# ⚓ Open Source Sailing Community

## A global community building the future of marine technology

Open Source Sailing is more than a collection of projects.

It is a community of sailors, makers, developers and innovators working together to create open and accessible marine technology.

---

# Who We Are

Our community brings together people with different backgrounds:

## ⛵ Sailors

People who use marine technology in real-world conditions and understand the challenges onboard.

## 💻 Developers

People who create software, integrations and digital solutions.

## 🔧 Makers & Engineers

People who design hardware, electronics and practical solutions.

## 📚 Educators & Learners

People who share knowledge and help others understand technology.

---

# How You Can Participate

Everyone can contribute.

You do not need to be a programmer or engineer.

Ways to participate:

- Share ideas
- Test prototypes
- Report problems
- Improve documentation
- Translate guides
- Create tutorials
- Develop software
- Design hardware
- Share sailing experiences

---

# Community Values

Our community is based on:

## Respect

Different skills, experiences and opinions make the project stronger.

## Collaboration

We solve problems together and share improvements.

## Openness

Knowledge should be available to everyone.

## Learning

Every experiment creates knowledge, including failures.

---

# Building Together

Open Source Sailing follows a build-in-public approach.

We share:

- Development progress
- Hardware experiments
- Software development
- Testing results
- Design decisions
- Lessons learned

The journey matters as much as the result.

---

# Community Platforms

The Open Source Sailing community grows through:

🌐 Website  
💻 GitHub  
💬 Discord  
🎥 YouTube  
❤️ Patreon  
📸 Social media

Each platform has a role:

- GitHub → development and collaboration
- Discord → discussions and support
- YouTube → education and storytelling
- Website → documentation and knowledge base
