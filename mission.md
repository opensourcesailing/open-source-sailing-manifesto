# ⚓ Open Source Sailing Mission

## Building open marine technology through collaboration

Open Source Sailing exists to develop, document and share open solutions for marine technology.

Our mission is to make sailing technology more accessible, understandable and sustainable by combining open-source software, open hardware and community knowledge.

---

# What We Do

Open Source Sailing focuses on five main areas:

## 💻 Open Software

We develop and promote open-source software solutions for sailing, including:

- Navigation systems
- Marine data platforms
- Automation tools
- Integration software
- Linux-based marine systems

---

## 🔧 Open Hardware

We design and document affordable marine hardware solutions:

- ESP32-based marine electronics
- Sensor interfaces
- Communication gateways
- Custom PCB designs
- 3D-printable components

---

## 🌊 Open Marine Data

We believe marine data should be accessible and interoperable.

We explore:

- NMEA0183
- NMEA2000
- Signal K
- AIS
- Weather data
- Sensor networks

---

## 📚 Education & Documentation

Knowledge should be shared.

We create:

- Technical documentation
- Tutorials
- Video guides
- Podcasts
- Workshops
- Community resources

---

## 🤝 Community Collaboration

Open Source Sailing connects:

- Sailors
- Developers
- Engineers
- Makers
- Researchers
- Students

Everyone can contribute through ideas, testing, documentation and development.

---

# Our Approach

We believe in:

- Building openly
- Documenting the process
- Sharing successes and failures
- Learning together
- Improving existing technology instead of replacing everything

---

# Our Commitment

Open Source Sailing will always work toward creating marine technology that is:

⚓ Open  
🔧 Repairable  
🌍 Sustainable  
🤝 Collaborative  
🚢 Accessible
